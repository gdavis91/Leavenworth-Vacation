About Leavenworth

Leavenworth is a charming Bavarian-style village in the Cascade Mountains, known for its scenic beauty, vibrant culture, and family-friendly atmosphere. It's a place where unforgettable memories are made.

Icicle Village Resort

Experience the best of Leavenworth at the Icicle Village Resort, offering a lounge, game room, swimming pool, hot tubs, and a mini putt course. The breathtaking mountain views will leave you speechless.

Best Burger in Town

Just across the street from the resort, you'll find a cozy burger joint serving the most delicious burgers in town. It's a must-visit spot for a quick and satisfying meal.

Explore Downtown Leavenworth

A short walk from the resort takes you to downtown Leavenworth, a bustling area filled with unique shops, great restaurants, and beautiful Bavarian architecture. Discover the charm that makes Leavenworth so special.
